What The App Does

ChitWise helps users personally track chit funds, installment payments, payouts, dividends, projected returns, reports, imports, exports, and backups.

The app does not provide loans, lending, brokerage, investment advice, credit products, or payment processing.

Data We Collect Or Store

When you use the app, the following data may be stored:

  • Account information: name, email address, sign-in provider, profile photo URL when provided by Google, selected language, and app settings.
  • Chit fund data: chit fund names, organizer names, type, frequency, contribution amount, total value, members, dates, status, and return calculation settings.
  • Installment data: installment number, payment date, amount paid, payout received, dividend, bid discount, notes, and timestamps.
  • Import/export and backup activity metadata: last export, backup, restore, and auto-backup status.

How Data Is Used

The data is used to show your chit fund dashboard and detail screens, calculate actual and projected returns, generate CSV and PDF exports, back up and restore your own app data, and keep your language and app settings consistent across sign-ins.

Where Data Is Stored

Live app data is stored in Firebase Firestore under your signed-in account.

Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication using email/password or Google sign-in.

If you use Google Drive backup, backup files are stored in your own Google Drive storage. Those files are separate from Firestore data.

Sharing

The app does not sell user data.

The app uses Firebase and Google services needed for authentication, database storage, Google sign-in, and optional Google Drive backup. Data may be processed by those services according to Google's terms and privacy policies.

CSV and PDF exports are created only when you request them. If you share exported files outside the app, that sharing is controlled by you.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and app data from Settings > Account > Danger zone > Delete account.

This deletes your Firebase profile, chit funds, installments, app settings, and Firebase Auth account. Google Drive backup files are stored in your own Drive and may need to be deleted from Drive separately.

You may also request deletion by contacting: support.jahathesh@gmail.com.

Security

Access to app data is restricted by Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules so users can access only their own records.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for adults managing personal financial records and is not directed to children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, storage, or legal requirements change. If the policy changes in a meaningful way, the app may ask you to review and accept the updated version.
