ChitWise Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: 26 May 2026

ChitWise users can delete their account and associated app data directly inside the app.

Delete your account in the app

Open ChitWise and go to:

Settings → Account → Danger zone → Delete account

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account and data deletion by emailing:

support.jahathesh@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Subject: ChitWise account deletion request
  • Your ChitWise account email address

Data deleted

After verification, we will delete your ChitWise account data, including:

  • Profile information
  • Chit fund records
  • Installment records
  • App settings
  • Import, export, backup, and restore activity metadata stored by the app

Google Drive backup files

Google Drive backup files are stored in your own Google Drive account. You may delete those files directly from Google Drive, or contact support for guidance.

Processing time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days.
